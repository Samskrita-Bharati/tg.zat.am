How to Play

Solve the स्वस्तिक-पदम् Puzzle in fewest moves possible.

You will earn a star for every move you have remaining once the svastika-padam is solved ⭐

Rearrange the letters into the correct words, horizontally and vertically. Drag letters anywhere on the board. The letters will change color to show whether they are in the correct position. The number of moves remaining is displayed below the board.
